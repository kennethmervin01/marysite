Transportation


Transportation will be provided from Canyons Village and the Waldorf Astoria on Saturday, July 9, 2016 to Blue Sky Ranch for the wedding ceremony and reception. Shuttles will be running intermittently as well so that guests may return to their hotels at the end of the evening.

Guests should arrive into Salt Lake City International Airport.

The travel time between Salt Lake City and Park city is approximately 40 minutes (33 miles).

We recommend renting a car (ranging between $35-$65 per day) but it's also possible to take a shuttle to the hotel.
